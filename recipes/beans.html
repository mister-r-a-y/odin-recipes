Delicious Black Beans

  • 28 oz. can of black beans
  • 1/2 of a red onion
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • white vinegar
  • kosher salt to taste

Steps

  1. Chop onion and jalapeno into rough slices.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of neutral oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Sautee the onions in the pot for 5 minutes--until soft. Then add the pepper to the pot and sautee for an additional few minutes. Do not let onions or peppers brown.
  3. Open can of beans and pour them into hot pot with their liquid.
  4. Add 2-3 tablespoons of white vinegar.
  5. Stir beans so that vegatables and vinegar are evenly distributed.
  6. Let beans cook in the pot for at least 6 minutes.
  7. (OPTIONAL): Use potato masher to mash beans into a mushier state.
